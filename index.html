About

The person behind the work

I am currently in the second year of my M.A. in English Literature at the University of Delhi, where I specialise in gender studies, media criticism, and the politics of representation. Before that, I studied at Gargi College, where I debated, organised, and learned to argue in public.

I have been writing since before I knew it was something you could do for a living. I started The Anti-Writer because I needed a place for things that did not fit neatly into academic prose — essays that are rigorous but do not perform their rigor.

I am available for writing, research assistance, fact-checking, script development, and editorial support. I work fast, take feedback well, and I am always happy to do a trial piece.
